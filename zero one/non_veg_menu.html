Chicken Biryani

Chicken Biryani is a traditional Indian rice dish renowned for its aromatic flavors and tender chicken pieces. It is a popular dish served across India and various other parts of the world, loved for its rich taste and fragrant spices. The dish typically consists of long-grain Basmati rice, marinated chicken, and a blend of spices such as cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, and bay leaves. To prepare Chicken Biryani, the chicken is first marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, which helps to tenderize the meat and infuse it with flavor.

Chicken Fried Rice

Chicken Fried Rice is a delightful Chinese-inspired dish featuring stir-fried rice tossed with tender chicken pieces and an array of colorful vegetables. The dish is flavored with soy sauce, garlic, ginger, and sometimes chili paste, imparting a savory and mildly spicy taste. It's a versatile meal that can be enjoyed on its own or as a side dish with other Asian delicacies. Chicken Fried Rice is quick and easy to prepare, making it a popular choice for busy weeknights or casual gatherings.

Mutton Biryani

Mutton Biryani is a luxurious and aromatic rice dish made with tender mutton pieces marinated in a blend of spices and yogurt. This traditional Indian delicacy is characterized by its rich flavors, fragrant aroma, and tender meat that melts in the mouth. The mutton is slow-cooked with layers of long-grain Basmati rice, caramelized onions, and a medley of spices, including cardamom, cinnamon, and cloves. The dish is often garnished with fried onions, chopped cilantro, and mint leaves, adding freshness and texture.

Rs.300

Fish Fry

This dish is a popular choice at seafood restaurants and eateries around the world, cherished for its crunchy texture and savory taste. Mixed Biryani

Mixed Biryani is a culinary masterpiece that combines the best of various worlds, blending the flavors of chicken, mutton, and prawns into a single, irresistible dish. Each bite is a symphony of tastes and textures, with tender mutton pieces, fragrant Basmati rice and aromatic spices. This indulgent dish begins with marinating the meats in a blend of spices and yogurt, infusing them with layers of flavor. The rice is parboiled with whole spices until partially cooked, ready to absorb the rich essence of the meats and spices.

Rs.250
